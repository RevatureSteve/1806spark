Mode 1 Mode 2 (trial)

How To Play:

Your goal is to find the hidden sequence of 4 numbers. Each number is randomly picked between 1 and 6. You have 9 guesses to find the sequence before its game over. With each guess, the game will tell you how many correct numbers you chose (if any) are in correct place and how many are in the wrong place.

How to Play:

Your goal, similar to before, is to find the sequence. This time you are provided with pre-made guesses and hints. You have unlimited attempts but you do not get any information about your guesses. There are only 3 numbers and each must be within 1 and 4.
