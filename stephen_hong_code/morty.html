Morty Smith

Main Aliases Morty
Species Human
Age 14
Status Alive
Job Student at Harry Herpson High School
Place of Origin Earth Dimension C-137

Morty Smith is one of the two main protagonists in Rick and Morty. He is the grandson of Rick and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures. Morty attends Harry Herpson High School along with his sister, Summer.
